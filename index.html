The Jack Russell Terrier is a small, energetic breed that loves to play and explore. They are fearless and brave, yet loyal and loving to their owners. They are intelligent, have high energy levels and need regular activity and mental stimulation. Jack Russells make great family pets, but do require regular exercise and training to ensure they remain well behaved and obedient. They are also known to be great watchdogs and are easily trained to do tricks.
